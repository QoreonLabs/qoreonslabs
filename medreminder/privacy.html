Privacy Policy

MedReminder by Qoreon Labs

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Your health data belongs to you, and you alone. Period.

MedReminder is built by Qoreon Labs with privacy as our core principle. We believe your personal health information should never leave your device, never be analyzed by algorithms, and never be sold to third parties.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data. Here's what that means:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No health data or medication details
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No tracking cookies or identifiers
  • No account creation or login required
  • No advertising IDs or third-party SDKs

If we don't collect it, we can't lose it, sell it, or misuse it. It's that simple.

Data Storage

All your medication information is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage (UserDefaults). Your data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have any!)
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is protected by iOS security measures

Think of MedReminder as a completely offline tool that happens to send you notifications.

Notifications

MedReminder uses iOS local notifications to remind you about medications. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled and processed entirely on your device
  • Do not send any data to external servers
  • Require your explicit permission to display
  • Can be managed through iOS Settings at any time

Local notifications are handled by iOS, not by any remote service. We never know when or if a notification was displayed.

Third-Party Services

MedReminder does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (Firebase, Sentry, etc.)
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage providers

The only third party involved is Apple, through the App Store distribution and iOS operating system.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you have enabled iCloud backup on your device, iOS may automatically back up MedReminder data to your personal iCloud account. This is:

  • Controlled by iOS system settings, not by MedReminder
  • Encrypted end-to-end by Apple
  • Accessible only to you through your Apple ID
  • Can be disabled in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage

We have no access to your iCloud data. This is entirely between you and Apple.

Children's Privacy

MedReminder can be used by children to track their medications with parental supervision. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children or anyone else
  • No age verification is required or performed
  • All data stays on the device, regardless of user age
  • Parents have full control over the app through device access

GDPR Compliance

MedReminder is GDPR Compliant by design.

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we collect no personal data:

  • Right to Access: No data to access
  • Right to Rectification: No data to correct
  • Right to Erasure: Simply delete the app
  • Right to Data Portability: Export manually or delete app
  • Right to Object: Nothing to object to

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Notify users through an in-app message
  • Publish the updated policy on this page
  • Never make changes that compromise your privacy

Our commitment to zero data collection will not change.
